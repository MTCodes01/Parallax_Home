Parallax Scrolling Test 2.0

Parallax scrolling is a popular web design technique that creates an illusion of depth and movement in a 2D environment, typically on a webpage. It achieves this effect by moving different layers of content at different speeds as you scroll down the page. The foreground elements move faster than the background, creating a sense of depth and immersion. It's like looking out of a moving car window, where objects closer to you appear to move faster than those in the distance.
